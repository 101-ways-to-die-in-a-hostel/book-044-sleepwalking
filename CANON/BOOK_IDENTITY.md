# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 044
**Title:** The Last Walk
**Slug:** sleepwalking
**Category:** Self-Inflicted

## The Death (One Sentence)
A chronic sleepwalker who downplays her condition walks off a fifth-floor balcony while dreaming of the beach she never got to visit, her known medical condition meeting a hostel's inadequate safety measures.

## Tagline
"She was already asleep when she took her last step."

## Themes
1. The danger of downplaying chronic conditions while traveling
2. How hostel infrastructure fails the most vulnerable guests
3. The eeriness of the unconscious mind making fatal decisions

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 20%
- Horror: 55%
- Satire: 25%

## Target Reader
Anyone who has a medical condition they don't take seriously while traveling. Travelers who've stayed in hostels with dubious safety features. People fascinated by sleep disorders and the bizarre things the unconscious mind can do.

## Comparison Titles
- *Sleepwalk With Me* meets *Final Destination*

## Content Warnings
- Fall from height
- Sleep disorder depiction
- Implicit death (not witnessed consciously by victim)
- Discussion of chronic illness management
